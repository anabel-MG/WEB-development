Introduction to group:Tomorrow X Together



TXT, also known as TOMORROW X TOGETHER, is a 5-member boy group formed by Big Hit Music (part of HYBE Labels) based in South Korea.
The group consists of: Yeonjun, Soobin, Beomgyu, Taehyun and Huening Kai.
They debuted on March 4th, 2019, with their first mini album ‘The Dream Chapter: STAR’.
There are no set positions within the group as it changes each comeback/song.
